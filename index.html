Right in the city of Lagos, TEJ Salon & Spa is the kind of place you actually look forward to visiting. It's not just about getting your hair done or your nails fixed. It's about taking time for yourself, being taken care of, and leaving better than you came in.

Our team knows their stuff. From natural hair and braids to facials, massages, and everything in between, we use only quality products that are good for your skin and hair and we take the time to do it right.
